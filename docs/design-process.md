# Design Process: PocketPace

## 1. The Design Timeline & Iteration History
The architectural roadmap of PocketPace highlights a deliberate transition from an empirical user interface experiment to a robust behavioral engineering framework:

1. **Phase 1: The Weekly Sandbox (Starting Line):** Utilizing Cashew as a testing canvas to validate that an isolated weekly progress bar and a "daily allowable spending" indicator successfully alters human spending choices at checkouts.
2. **Phase 2: Identifying the Leakage Flaw:** Recognizing that unexpected, non-weekly variable expenses corrupt the weekly signal, inducing user drop-off.
3. **Phase 3: Architecting the 3-Tier Model:** Evolving the backend logic to feature a hidden monthly buffer and automated surplus splitting to insulate the weekly "game".

## 2. Naming Iteration & The Veto Log
Before arriving at the final branding, multiple creative paths were explored to anchor a design vocabulary that implies "money balance" without triggering "institutional stress":

* **The Rhythm/Music Phase (Vetoed):** Terms like *Tempo, Cadence,* and *Stride* were thoroughly mapped to evoke life rhythms. They were vetoed due to a high semantic risk of users mistaking the application for a music streaming service or step-tracking fitness app.
* **The Corporate-Verb Phase (Vetoed):** Terms like *CashStride* and *BudgetFlow* were attempted. They were quickly vetoed because words like "Budget" and "Cash" re-introduced the rigid, corporate "Accounting Anxiety" the project was explicitly trying to defeat.
* **The Selection (Pocket + Pace):** The breakthrough occurred by establishing the subtitle first: *"Your Money, at your own pace."* The word **"Pocket"** was locked in to evoke childhood "pocket money"—representing small, highly manageable, non-threatening containers of resource. **"Pace"** was added to celebrate individual lifestyle rhythms.

## 3. Brand Identity & Visual Specifications
* **Iconography:** Standard industrial wallet and credit card vectors were rejected. The locked design is a **modernized drawstring pouch (👝)**—a historical money-vessel re-imagined as a clean personal bag.
* **The Wave Logic:** Soft, minimalist **wavy lines** are woven into the pouch asset, explicitly signaling that the resources inside are fluid, dynamic, and moving in pacing waves rather than sitting as a static, frozen stack.
* **Color Psychology:** The application interface rejects clinical, stressful banking blues and greens. It adopts an optimistic, energetic **Orange** as its dominant brand color to evoke warmth and positive habit reinforcement.
